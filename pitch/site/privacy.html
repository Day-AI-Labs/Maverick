Privacy & cookies

How this website handles cookies and personal data. The short version: it sets no tracking cookies, runs no analytics, and profiles no one.

Last updated 18 June 2026

Cookies

This website sets no cookies — no tracking cookies, no advertising cookies, no analytics cookies. We verified this: the site returns no Set-Cookie headers. Because nothing non-essential is stored on your device, there is nothing to consent to, which is why you won't find a cookie banner here. If we ever introduce a cookie that isn't strictly necessary, we will ask for your consent first and document it on this page.

Analytics & tracking

None. We do not run Google Analytics or any other analytics or advertising tool, we do not fingerprint your browser, and we do not build a profile of your visit.

Third-party services

Two services are involved in delivering this site. Neither sets cookies, but both necessarily process a connecting IP address to do their job:

  • Cloudflare — hosts and serves the site (Cloudflare Pages) and provides transport security. Cloudflare processes standard request metadata, including IP address, to deliver the page securely.
  • Google Fonts — the site's typefaces currently load from Google Fonts, so your browser requests the font files from Google, which may log the requesting IP address. We are moving these fonts to first-party hosting to remove that transfer; once that ships, this line goes away.

Information you give us

If you email us — for example, the Book a demo or Request access links, which open your own mail client — we receive whatever you send (your email address and message) and use it solely to respond to you. We don't sell it, rent it, or share it with anyone outside Daybreak Labs, and we keep it only as long as we need it to handle your request.

The Lightwork product vs. this website

This policy covers the marketing website only. The Lightwork platform itself is self-hosted on your own infrastructure and runs egress-locked, so operational data processed by the product stays inside your environment — it does not flow back to us.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example, under the GDPR or CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you, and to object to its processing. To exercise any of these, email info@daybreakailabs.com and we'll respond within the timeframe the law requires.

Contact

Questions about this policy, or about privacy at Daybreak Labs, go to info@daybreakailabs.com. We may update this page as the site evolves; the date at the top reflects the latest version.
