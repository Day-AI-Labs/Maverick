# What this skill does

Drafts a sustainability/ESG report aligned to a chosen framework: material topics, metrics with methodology, targets and progress, and defensible claims.

# Steps

1. Confirm the reporting framework (GRI, SASB, ISSB/TCFD) and the reporting boundary and period — claims must trace to a standard.
2. Run or summarize the materiality basis and report only material topics; for each, give the metric, methodology, and data source from `spreadsheet`.
3. State targets and progress honestly, including where the company is behind. Separate operational data from estimates and label assured vs unassured figures.
4. Scrub for greenwashing: every claim needs evidence and a boundary. State methodology and hand off for assurance/legal review.

# Notes

ESG reports create legal exposure when claims outrun evidence or boundaries are fuzzy. Conservative, sourced, boundaried claims only. Public sustainability claims need legal and assurance sign-off — this skill drafts, it does not publish.
