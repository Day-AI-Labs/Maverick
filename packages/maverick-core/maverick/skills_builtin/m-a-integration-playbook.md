# What this skill does

Builds a post-merger integration playbook: Day-1 readiness, the 100-day plan by function, synergy tracking, and the decisions that need an owner before close.

# Steps

1. Define the integration thesis and operating model decision (absorb / preserve / best-of-both) — every downstream choice flows from it.
2. Build Day-1 readiness by function: payroll, systems access, customer/vendor comms, legal entity, and the must-not-break list.
3. Lay out the 100-day plan with workstream owners, milestones, and dependencies; attach synergy targets (cost and revenue) with a tracking mechanism.
4. Name the people and culture risks and the retention plan for key talent. Surface decisions needing an owner. State assumptions and hand off.

# Notes

Integrations fail on culture and key-talent flight more than on systems. Don't let synergy spreadsheets crowd out the people plan. Org and comp decisions are leadership's — this skill structures them.
