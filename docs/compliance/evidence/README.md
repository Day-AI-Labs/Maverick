# Compliance Evidence

Operating records produced by *running* the procedures — the output an auditor
inspects to confirm the ISMS/AIMS is not just designed but **operating**. Unlike
the policies/procedures (which say what to do), these are dated records of what
was actually done.

| Record | Type | Date | Source procedure |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [2026-Q2 Internal Audit Report](2026-Q2-internal-audit-report.md) | Internal audit (Cl. 9.2) | 2026-06-24 | [PROC-05](../procedures/internal-audit-plan.md) |
| [2026-06-24 Management Review](2026-06-24-management-review.md) | Management review (Cl. 9.3) | 2026-06-24 | [TPL-02](../templates/management-review-minutes-template.md) |

These initial records are **Draft — prepared for ratification**: the internal
audit was conducted against the codebase + live `maverick soc2` posture; the
management review is pre-filled from current state for the owner to conduct and
sign. As the program operates, add: `maverick soc2 --json` posture snapshots
(dated, through the Type II window), incident reports, completed vendor reviews,
training-completion records, and subsequent quarterly audits/reviews.

> Keep dated evidence here (or in your evidence store) for the audit window. The
> [hardening checklist](../deployment/hardening-checklist.md) §5 lists the
> technical evidence to retain.
