This is the **template** for recording the management review required by ISO 27001
/ ISO 42001 **Clause 9.3**. The management review is held **at least annually**
(and after a major incident or change). The completed minutes are a controlled
record and a primary input to certification. Copy this file per meeting and fill
every section — required inputs (§4) and outputs (§5) **must** all be addressed;
record "none" explicitly rather than omitting a heading.

## 4. Required inputs (Clause 9.3.2)

### 4.1 Changes in internal & external issues

Relevant to the ISMS/AIMS — org/structure changes, new products or deployment
contexts, new/changed regulation (e.g. EU AI Act developments), threat-landscape
shifts. Reference PROC-04 trigger-based re-assessments raised this period.

_…_

### 4.2 ISMS/AIMS performance & effectiveness

- **Information-security & AI incidents** — count, severity, themes, time-to-close
  (POL-07 records).
- **Internal audit results** — findings by class (Major / Minor / Observation)
  from [PROC-05](../procedures/internal-audit-plan.md); closure rate.
- **Risk status** — movement in the [Risk Register](../risk-register.md);
  residuals above appetite; new risks; AIRA outcomes (methodology §6).
- **Objectives / KPI progress** — security & AI objectives vs. target.
- **Control posture** — output of `maverick soc2` (automated control/evidence
  status) and any control regressions vs. the SoAs
  ([ISO 27001](../iso-27001/statement-of-applicability.md),
  [ISO 42001](../iso-42001/statement-of-applicability.md)).
- **Monitoring & measurement** — uptime/availability, budget/cost controls,
  vulnerability remediation SLAs.

_…_

### 4.3 Feedback from interested parties

Customers, auditors, regulators, partners, affected third parties (ISO 42001).
Include relevant feedback, complaints, contractual/security-questionnaire themes.

_…_

### 4.4 Results of risk assessment & status of risk treatment

Outcome of the latest quarterly / annual assessment (PROC-04); treatment plan
progress; residual-risk acceptance decisions requested of management.

_…_

### 4.5 Opportunities for continual improvement

Improvement opportunities raised from audits (Observations), incidents,
near-misses, the risk process, and staff suggestions.
