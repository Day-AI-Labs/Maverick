# Maverick Summit v2 (hybrid) — delta kit

**Roadmap ref:** 2028-H1 "Maverick Summit v2 (hybrid)". This is a **delta
over [summit-v1-virtual.md](./summit-v1-virtual.md)** — read that first;
everything there (agenda template, speaker brief, streaming/Q&A checklists,
metrics, artifact plan) still applies. Running the event is a maintainer
act; dates/venue/budget are founder-set.

## What changes for hybrid

1. **One event, two front rows.** The in-person room and the stream get
   equal standing: every talk has an online Q&A lane with its own moderator
   (don't make remote attendees shout into a room mic), and the hallway
   track gets an online counterpart (topic channels opened per block).
2. **Venue adds** (over v1's streaming checklist): a venue with hardline
   uplink + backup bonded cellular; an A/V vendor contracted for camera +
   room audio into the same encoder pipeline v1 specifies; confidence
   monitors showing the stream so speakers see what remote attendees see.
3. **Capacity + registration.** In-person capped by venue (founder-set);
   stream uncapped. One registration flow, two ticket types; the in-person
   ticket may be paid (founder-set) while the stream stays free — pricing
   must not paywall the content itself (recordings publish per v1).
4. **Schedule for two time zones, not one.** Pick the block that overlaps
   the two largest community regions from the community survey data; repeat
   the keynote as a re-stream with live chat for the third region rather
   than pretending one slot serves everyone.
5. **Code of Conduct** applies in-room and online identically
   (CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md); name the in-room contact and the online contact in
   the opening slide.

## Hybrid-specific run-of-show inserts

- T-minus 10 min per talk: A/V vendor confirms room feed in the encoder;
  online moderator posts the talk thread.
- During: room questions repeated into the mic *always* (the stream can't
  hear the room); online questions get equal rotation.
- After each block: hallway-track prompts posted online; room signage
  points at the same threads.

## Budget deltas over v1

| Line | Note |
|---|---|
| Venue + insurance | founder-set; the single biggest new cost |
| A/V vendor | camera, room audio, encoder op — do not volunteer-staff this |
| Captioning | live captioner or vendor; v1's auto-caption fallback is the floor |
| Travel grants | optional, founder-set; prioritize speakers over staff |

## Success metrics (over v1's)

- Remote-attendee talk-completion rate within 10% of in-person dwell
- ≥1 question per talk from each audience
- Post-event survey split by attendance mode (the v1 survey + one field)
