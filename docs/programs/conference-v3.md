# Lightwork Conference v3 (in-person flagship) — delta kit

**Roadmap ref:** 2028-H2 "Lightwork Conference v3 (in-person flagship)".
Deltas over [summit-v2-hybrid.md](./summit-v2-hybrid.md) (which itself
deltas v1) — the run-of-show, A/V, CoC, and metrics machinery all carry
forward. Running it is a maintainer act; everything money- or date-shaped
is founder-set.

## What "flagship" adds

1. **Two days, two tracks.** Track A: users (the season-2/3 video material
   live: working loop, team ops). Track B: builders (season-4 material:
   plugins, federation, benchmarks). The hybrid stream from v2 carries
   track A in full; track B records and publishes next-day (one stream
   crew, honest about it).
2. **A real CFP.** Public call, the speaker brief from v1 as the
   acceptance contract, review by maintainers + two community reviewers
   from the ambassadors program; every submission gets a verdict.
3. **Hands-on lab.** A half-day room running the curriculum kit
   ([curriculum-kit.md](./curriculum-kit.md)) labs against the demo
   cluster blueprint — laptops + the seeded environment, TAs from the
   ambassador bench.
4. **The hallway is the product.** Unstructured time ≥ 35% of the clock;
   v2's online hallway threads run alongside.
5. **State of Lightwork keynote** anchored in artifacts, not adjectives:
   the year's FEATURES delta, the benchmark trend from `/benchmarks`, the
   survey results, and the next-year roadmap — the same evidence rules as
   the press kit (no claims the catalogue can't back).

## Go/no-go gate (set before announcing)

Announce only when: v2 (hybrid) actually ran and its survey returned
mode-split satisfaction within target; sponsorship covers the venue floor
cost without ticket dependence; and two maintainers + the safety contact
can attend in person. Otherwise run v2-format again — a flagship that
strains the team is worse than no flagship.
