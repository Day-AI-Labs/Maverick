# Lightwork 2029–2031 — forward plan

The successor frame to the 2026–2028 roadmap (whose code-buildable backlog is
now substantially shipped — see `FEATURES.md`). Deliberately coarser than the
per-half tables: three themes per year, refined by the council cadence as
real usage data accrues. This is the planning artifact the 2028-H2 "3-year
retrospective + 2029-2031 plan" item calls for; the retrospective halves run
on their period generators (`safety_report`, `benchmark_retrospective`) when
the time elapses.

## 2029 — Consolidation & scale
- **Hosted control plane GA**: the multi-tenant spine (tenancy, billing,
  quotas, federation) hardened against live infra — Postgres RLS, real Redis
  brokers, worker pools — the items the 2026 tables marked live-infra-gated.
- **Model-trained safety members**: replace the heuristic Shield-v3 ensemble
  members with trained ones once trajectory volume crosses the learning-
  substrate tripwire (docs/specs/learning-substrate-decision.md).
- **Native surfaces where the measurement justifies**: fund the TS client iff
  the Q1-2027 language gate fired; desktop/mobile shells iff channel
  analytics show the demand.

## 2030 — Ecosystem maturity
- Marketplace federation + certification at volume; plugin CA adoption as the
  publishing floor; skill economy (the donate-direct model proven or cut).
- Federation GA: cross-org swarm delegation with the audit reciprocity model
  as the compliance story.
- LTS cadence locked (2-year safety branches; sunset policy enforced by CI).

## 2031 — The retrospective year
- Run the 3-year retrospectives (perf: benchmark_retrospective over the full
  recorded span; safety: safety_report annuals composed); publish them.
- Re-derive the next 3-year frame from measured reality, not aspiration.

## Standing constraints (carry-forward)
Self-host first · users own model choice · budget caps non-optional ·
sandbox-mediated shell · no new dependency without a knob · the wizard is the
UX source of truth.
